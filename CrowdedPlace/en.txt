8. Your continued use of Crowded Place will be deemed your acceptance of our privacy policy. If you have any questions about how we process user data, do not hesitate to contact us.
                        
9. Age restriction: 18 years old or majority according to local laws.

10. This policy is effective from May 21, 2021.
